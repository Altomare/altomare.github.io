### User's Guides

User guides were distributed in binders with individual chapters to fill them.
Here is the usual configurations:
* Attaché Volume 1
  * Operator's Guide
  * Software Guide
  * CP/M Guide
* Attaché Volume 2
  * Wordstar-Plus Guide
  * Valet Guide
  * Charton Guide
* 8-16 Volume 1
  * Introduction
  * 8-16 Operator's Guide
  * MS-DOS Guide
